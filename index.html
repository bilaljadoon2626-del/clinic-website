FAQs

Common questions from patients

Do I need an appointment, or do you accept walk-ins?

We recommend booking ahead through our appointment form for the shortest wait, but walk-ins are always welcome for urgent concerns and emergency care.

Which insurance plans does LifeCare accept?

We work with most major insurance providers. Call our front desk with your plan details and we'll confirm coverage before your visit.

Can I choose a specific doctor for my appointment?

Yes — our booking form lets you select a preferred doctor. If they're unavailable at your requested time, we'll offer the nearest openings.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant prior medical records.

Is emergency care available outside office hours?

Yes, our emergency line is staffed 24/7. Call our emergency number directly and a clinician will guide you through next steps.

How soon will I get my lab results?

Most standard panels are ready within 24–48 hours. Your doctor or a nurse will call you directly to walk through the results.
